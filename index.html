introduction

Hi! I am Sepand Assadi. Full Stack Software Engineer Based in San Francisco.

about

I was born in Iran, studied software engineering in Cyprus, and, inspired by computers and technology, moved to the US in 2008. In order to establish myself here, I went into marketing, sales, and management.

In my 4 years at Verizon Wireless, I trained Store Managers and Sales Specialists for over 15 stores and became Best Manager of the Year. We surpassed revenue-per-sale goals by over 20% and 5 of my trainees went on to get promotions (4 of which were in management).

Moving towards my passion for technology, I transitioned into IT management, where I was the IT go to guy for fast-paced, rapidly growing, startup-cultured environments of 50+ users over 3 sites.

Eventually, I found my way to Dev Bootcamp, where I learned how to be a more productive and efficient software engineer. As a software developer, I love the feeling of being confused, knowing that the very next thing is a better understanding of that topic or language. I am strongly focused on usability, functionality, code quality and design.

My interests and specialities include: Ruby on Rails, JavaScript, CSS, Sass, Bootstrap, Materialize, SQL, Swift (Beginner), Python (Beginner), Angular.JS and MEAN Stack (Beginner).

I'm always eager to learn more and become a better software developer. Do you think I would make a good addition to your developer's team? Shoot me an email at email or visit my LinkedIn profile for almost a full resume.
